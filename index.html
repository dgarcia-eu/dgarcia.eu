David Garcia

Professor for Social and Behavioral Data Science, University of Konstanz

I am a computer scientist working on Social Data Science. My goal is to understand human behavior using large-scale data and computational models, with a focus on emotions, mental health, and online interactions.

Research

I work at the Complexity Science Hub Vienna and collaborate with the Medical University of Vienna and Graz University of Technology. My research connects computational social science with responsible AI.
